About Me

With a background primarily rooted in the construction industry as a Purchaser/Procurement Officer, I have honed valuable skills in interpersonal communication, canvassing, and inventory management. While appreciative of the opportunities this field has provided, I've discovered my true passion lies within the IT industry, particularly as a Developer/Programmer.

Excited about transitioning to this new field, I aim to leverage my unique perspective and skills to contribute to its success. Despite lacking direct experience, I am confident in my transferable skills, adaptability, and eagerness to learn, which I believe will facilitate my success in this career shift.

I have already taken steps to obtain the Certificate of Competency in Web Development NC III , and I am motivated to educate myself further about this field, including ASP.Net MVC, .Net Core, and Microsoft SQL Server.

Moreover, I currently serve as the administrator of web app-related accounts, tasked with extracting crucial data necessary for generating special reports required by the National TB Control Program. These responsibilities are integral to the maintenance and oversight of web applications associated with Tuberculosis (TB). In this capacity, I utilize various technologies including PHP, MySQL, CodeIgniter, XAMPP, jQuery, and REST API to develop and maintain the Integrated Tuberculosis Information System (ITIS). Furthermore, I am responsible for managing the National Tuberculosis Control Program using WordPress.

I am eager to continue learning and growing in this dynamic field, committed to making meaningful contributions while expanding my expertise.
