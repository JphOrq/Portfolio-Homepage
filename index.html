Won-Now

The "Wheel of Ninong-Ninang on Wheels" is a popular game in the Philippines because it is a fun and easy way for "Ninong/Ninang (godparents)" to give gifts to their "Inaanak (godchildren)". It is also a good way for "Ninong/Ninang" to bond with their "Inaanak" and create lasting memories. The game is played by spinning a wheel that is divided into different sections, each with a different prize. The player wins the prize that is in the section where the wheel lands.
